OFFICE OF THE CONTROLLER EXAMINATIONS

The Controller of Examinations oversees the examination ensuring its integrity and efficiency. Key responsibilities include:


Examination Management

  • Preparing, scheduling, and conducting End Semester Examinations and Supplementary Examinations
  • Facilitating Central evaluation of answer scripts
  • Processing and publishing of results as per procedures laid down by the University

Student Support Services

  • Providing timely notifications to departments regarding examination processes
  • Facilitating paper revaluation and viewing
  • Issuing grade sheets, Statements of marks, Percentage Certificates, Transcripts and Provisional Degree / Diploma Certificates to eligible candidates
  • Verifying and attestation of academic documents
  • Providing duplicate certificates
